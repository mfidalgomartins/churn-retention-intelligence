# Dashboard Section Decision Support

## 1. Header + Filters
**Decision supported**: Which slice of the business should leadership focus on right now?
- Enables leadership to isolate underperforming regions, channels, plans, and risk tiers before acting.

## 2. Executive Summary Strip
**Decision supported**: What are the 4-6 most important takeaways from the current filter scope?
- Provides immediate focus areas (concentration, value leakage, warning signals, and operational risk).

## 3. KPI Row
**Decision supported**: Is retention health improving, stable, or deteriorating, and how urgent is intervention?
- Quantifies scale (`active`), performance (`customer/revenue churn`), and urgency (`revenue at risk`, `critical risk`, `avg priority`).

## 4. Primary Retention Section
**Decision supported**: Are churn and retention structural or temporary?
- Trend charts support pacing and timing decisions.
- Cohort views support decisions on onboarding quality and lifecycle strategy.

## 5. Diagnostic Section
**Decision supported**: What is driving churn by customer profile and behavior?
- Dimension charts direct segment/channel/plan-level accountability.
- Usage/support/payment/NPS diagnostics identify preventable operational causes.

## 6. Risk Prioritization Section
**Decision supported**: Which accounts should be contacted first, and why?
- Ranked table supports immediate account-level execution.
- Risk tier distribution and driver mix support staffing and playbook allocation.
- Segment revenue risk chart supports where to allocate leadership attention for maximum revenue protection.

## 7. Action Section
**Decision supported**: Which intervention motions should be staffed and sequenced this cycle?
- Groups accounts by recommended actions (`billing`, `CS outreach`, `adoption`, `renewal save`, `monitor`).
- Provides MRR coverage by action to prioritize resource deployment.

## 8. Footer (Methodology/Caveats)
**Decision supported**: How should leadership interpret this dashboard responsibly?
- Clarifies scoring logic, proxy limitations, and refresh discipline for governance and trust.
